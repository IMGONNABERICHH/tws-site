About TWS

An independent music & culture publication, born in Los Angeles▓

TWS covers the artists and moments shaping music culture from the ground up — live event coverage, artist interviews, festival reporting, and radio. Our lens is the LA underground and the culture the rest of the industry follows years too late.

Our coverage spans Grammy-week events, Coachella, festival launches, and one-on-one interviews with rising and established artists — documented in original video, photography, and writing.
