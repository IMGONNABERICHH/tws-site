Marina Sakimoto, better known by her stage name Shunkan, is a Grammy-nominated producer, singer, and songwriter. She just dropped the single Hell-Bound, the first taste of her upcoming album Kamikaze Girl, which is set to release on May 6, 2025. The track blends soft drum beats with classic rock guitar, creating a vibe that takes you straight back to the ‘90s alternative rock era. But Hell-Bound is more than just a nostalgic song; it really shows off Shunkan’s artistry and versatile cadence, giving us a glimpse of what’s to come on the album. Offering a glimpse into the sonic world she's created for the album. And even though Kamikaze Girl will be her third album, it’s her first in five years, making this a highly anticipated return.
