Nia Sultana is a alternative RnB singer and songwriter from Brooklyn, New York. Sultana has recently dropped her debut EP called Bigger Dreams via Interscope Records . The tracks are dreamy, mesmerizing, and melodic. Sultana's EP also features notable artist like Cheru Ecru and Rick Ross. Whom effortlessly compliment each other on Sultana's EP.
