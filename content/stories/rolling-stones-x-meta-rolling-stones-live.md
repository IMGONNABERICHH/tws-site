On Saturday April 16th Rolling Stone and Meta held an event surrounding Coachella called Rolling Stone Live.Rolling Stone Live featured musical performances from Grace Mackgan, BLXST, Carwash, James Hype and GRYFFIN with special guest DJ performances by Densine Love Hewett, and Tolula Adeyemi.
Live also featured eye catching photo installments by META formerly known as Facebook. While Lucky Brand and Get Stoned curated a tattoo and screen printing pop up shop for the attendees called "Fantastic Future".Live also included multiple open bar spaces from brands like Ciroc, Matua, and a pop up from Madre's Restaurant. All in the beautiful desert of Palm Springs , California.
