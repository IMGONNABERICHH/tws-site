Straight out of Houston, Texas, BBY Kodie has been making waves. BBY Kodie's rhythmic and catchy tunes make him hard to ignore. On his songs like “Milkshake'' sampled from Kelis 2003 hit song "Milkshake".He explores and defines his upbeat and dynamic flow as an artist. That same energy was seen during BBY Kodie's recent performance at the Regent Theatre. For The Zack Fox & Friends show on Saturday July 24th.BBYKODIE made the crowd go wild with every song.By moshing, moonwalking, and natural stage presence.If you ever catch him on your local line up go see for yourself. He is a definite artist to watch.
