Shenseea, formerly known as Chinsea Lee, is a 25-year-old Jamaican dancehall artist who has become one of the biggest names in the genre. Shenseea is most known for her catchy rhythms and high-energy music She even recently released her album ALPHA, which is gaining attention worldwide.Throughout her career, Shenseea has worked with major artists like Drake, 21 Savage, Kanye West (Ye), and Megan Thee Stallion. These collaborations have helped her gain recognition beyond the dancehall scene.Her latest music video for the song "R U That," featuring 21 Savage, has also been a hit, showcasing her unique style.Shenseea's growth and development as an artist is inspiring to see, proving she's a game-changer in the dancehall scene
