Rap Star Energy, IHEARTRADIO, BigIndy, 105.3, and the Hip-Hop rapper Rick Ross are all coming together for an event called "The Promise Land Car & Bike Show." The show will take place in Fayetteville, GA on the 21st of May.At the show there will be a talent showcase where artist, dancers, comedians, and other talent will showcase their skills. At the end of the showcase the best performances will be able to compete in the Ultimate Performance. The best preformer will win endorsement opportunities, a large sum of money and a chance to headline at RapStar Energy's major events in the future. The car show will be hosted specifically by Rick Ross on his property in Georgia called "The Promised Land. " Ross's love for Bikes and cars exotic, vintage, and modern brought the dream the Car & Bike showcase to life.
