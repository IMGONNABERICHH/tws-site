Eladio Carrión, 30, is a Puerto Rican singer-songwriter who was born in Kansas City, Kansas. He recently released Don Kbrn, a twenty two track Latin trap album featuring major artists like Big Sean, Jessie Reyez, and Peso Pluma.The project blends hard-hitting trap drums with unique synths, giving the album an eccentric and dynamic sound. On tracks like “Mosh Pit Musik,” the production has a clear direction and energy that pulls the listener in and lets them fully immerse themselves in the experience.Overall Don Kbrn feels like the perfect project to cruise to in the summer.
