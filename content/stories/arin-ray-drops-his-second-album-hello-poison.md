Arin Ray, 26, singer-songwriter, has recently dropped his highly anticipated album Hello Poison via Interscope Records. With this new release, Ray continues to captivate listeners with his silky, groovy tone over laid-back drum patterns. Hello Poison features collaborations with Ari Lennox, D Smoke, VanJess, Blxst, Ty Dolla $ign, Rose Gold, and Childish Major. Ray has already proven his success, accumulating over 175 million streams in the U.S. alone with his previous album, Platinum Fire (2018), and his EP Phrases II (2019). Ray no signs of slowing down as hw continues to push boundaries and elevate his sound.
