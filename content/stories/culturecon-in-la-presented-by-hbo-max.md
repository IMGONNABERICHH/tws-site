On Saturday, June 17th for the first time Culture Con LA is having their event at Rolling Greens Mateo in Downtown Los Angeles. The date was chosen to celebrate the Juneteenth weekend. The panelist included Victoria Monét, Coco Jones, Jay Pharoah, Sterling K. Brown, The Blackening cast and many more. The panelist went over their success as an artist, advice to the audience, and business structures. Culture-con also had a few special surprises such as a performance from Lakeyah, as she rapped "Mind Yo business. Which got the members of the audience hyped and out their seat. As she walked into the crowd for a more interactive experience. Overall, the event was a mix of being both entertaining and informative.
