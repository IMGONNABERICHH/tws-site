Malik Yusef is a Multi-Grammy award winning writer, composer, director, poet, and producer. As well the founder of Bad Kid's Klass, "an annual writing and production camp for placements, playlists, and radio." (BadKidsKollection)Yusef, has not only reached high accolades, but is also very humble and continues to strive for more in his profession. Mentioning that he is at a point in his life where he is starting to "pick the right people to love." A strong message in such an egotistical world. It is always important to spread love while continuing to strive for more out of life.The strive for more mentality translates into Yusef's strong work ethic.Which landed Yusef an impressive 10 nominations this year. Although, Yusef ultimately won one Grammy for his work on Kanye West's album "DONDA" for the song "Jail" including Kanye West and Jay - Z . He continues to break the mold in a profession few people last in. Yusef has continued to put his best foot forward and is now a 8 time Grammy award winner with more to come.
