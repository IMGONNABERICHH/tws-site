Dope Dev, now known as Iamyourlittlebrother, is a rapper, producer, and songwriter based in Houston, Texas. He often collaborates with long-time producer and artist Indica Nights, and together, they craft a unique sound that blends together some classic elements of hip hop which is rooted deeply in rhyme and intricate use of sampling. 
Which shows in their recent visuals for their track called “Possible” which plays out like a back and forth conversation between the two artists. The visualizer showcases an intimate moment where the two artists interact, drawing the listener into their world. The concept of the video feels like eavesdropping on a private exchange between the two. Capturing a feeling of being on the outside, yet completely staying in the loop.
