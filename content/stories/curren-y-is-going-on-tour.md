Shante Franklin, better known as Curren$y, is hitting the road this spring for his highly anticipated 4:20 Tour with The 747 Band. The tour kicks off on April 18, 2025 in Austin, Texas, and to other cities across the country. The tour follows the release of his latest album which dropped on March 14. The project is a collaboration with notable producer Harry Fraud and showcases an intriguing blend of classic hip-hop with Curren$y's signature hard hitting flow over trap-style drums.A highlight of the tour will take place on April 22, 2025. When Curren$y performs at Warehouse Live Midtown venue in Houston, Texas. As the opening the night act is rappers, producer, and of 7 L'ANGES close collaborator, Iamyourlittlebrother.Overall, the 4:20 Tour is slated to provide an authentic musical experience with live instrumentation top-tier performances.
