What better way to start Coachella then a pre-party ?Yes, that's right. NTWRK and Galore threw a Coachella pre-party in Palms Springs, CA with an open bar. The party featured NYX glam stations where the onsite NYX makeup artist were available to create stunning festival makeup looks for attendees. The party also featured host Tyga (Rapper) who did a DJ set at the party and Actor Chloe Cherry (Euphoria).There was also an onsite Foot Locker pop up shops showcasing streetwear designers Melody Ehshani and Don C's latest collections.While vintage fashion designer Sean Weatherspoon also stopped by the party and went live via The NTWRK App. In order to showcase his latest collection called "Late Lunch". While rapper Freddie Gibbs and NTWRK had a logo ice cream truck set up satisfying enough for each sweet tooth. Overall the NTWRK and Galore Pre-party was one of the best ways to start Weekend 1 of Coachella. Due to its upbeat and energetic nature.
