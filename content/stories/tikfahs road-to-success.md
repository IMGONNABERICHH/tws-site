Tifkah, a Louisiana artist now based in Los Angeles, introduced herself to the world with her debut mixtape, "Say Red", released on October 22nd, 2024. The mixtape effortlessly blends upbeat, catchy melodies that pull you in from the very first track. Standout songs like “BLD” and “Louisiana (Intro)” highlight Tifkah’s unique style and her ability to deliver quality music. “BLD” features an infectious rhythm paired with her confident flow, while “Louisiana (Intro)” pays tribute to her Southern roots, seamlessly integrating a modern twist. With "Say Red", Tifkah is firmly establishing herself as an artist to watch. If this debut is any indication, she’s definitely one to keep an eye on in the future.
