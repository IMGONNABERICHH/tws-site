The 24 year old Toronto based artist " Mustafa The Poet" formally known as Mustafa Ahmed. Preformed his debut album "When Smoke Rises" released on May 28, 2021. At the Goya Studios in Hollywood, California. Mustafa's authentically rooted Islamic and Folk sound was sweet and melodic . Each song was poised with raw emotion. Which led into a cry mid performance. Mustafa stated "When Smoke Rises" ' is a "dedicated eulogy'' to his close friends that have passed away.His strength to evoke those reminiscent memories and feelings of his close friends carried throughout his performances. Mustafa's lyrics are relatable to anyone that has ever lost anyone close to them. Each word holds value. Mustafa's originality and uplifting tone makes his music compelling. In all Mustafa an artist to keep an eye out for.
