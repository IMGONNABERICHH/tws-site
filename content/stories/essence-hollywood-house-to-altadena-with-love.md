kicker: Event
location: Los Angeles

Essence Hollywood House is a dynamic space where like-minded creatives, artists, and professionals can come together to enhance their skills and knowledge in an ever-evolving industry. By connecting top-tier experts with aspiring artists, it creates a strong sense of community and belonging, reminding everyone that regardless of their background, they have an equal opportunity to succeed in the entertainment industry.
One example of this is the speed mentoring program, which gives aspiring artists the chance to learn directly from successful professionals. This program not only helps them grow but gives them the tools to fully express their creativity. Additionally, there will be a live workshop led by industry-leading film professionals, designed to teach actors essential skills needed to thrive on set. Beyond supporting individual growth, Essence Hollywood House also recognizes the importance of giving back to the community. The event shines a light on Altadena, a community deeply impacted by the wildfires in January 2025. Altadena has long been a vital hub for economic growth and creative collaboration within the Black professional community. To bring attention to this, one of the day’s panels, “Hollywood & Altadena: A Shared Legacy”, presented by AT&T Dream In Black, will explore the city’s significance and its strong connection to Hollywood. This is just one of many thought-provoking discussions taking place throughout the day.
