As Covid-19 cases have dropped and Los Angeles has almost came back to normal. I have seen a shift in concert culture. As more NFT concerts are starting to happen. Starting with Last weekend at TheWeatherFest.
TheWeatherFest was located at the Wisdome LA in Downtown Los Angeles. The Fest showcased artist such as Buddy, YG, Soulja Boy, Blondie Beach, Pedro and special guest Rae Sremmurd. The Fest was free for rsvp and V.I.P for NFT holders along with swag bags. The fest contained a very high artist production value all while making a pleasing experience to most. Which make me believe that NFT Festivals will change concert life as the way we know it for years to come.
