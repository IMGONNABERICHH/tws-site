Nicki Nicole formally known as Nicole Cucco is a 21 year old rapper, songwriter, and singer from Argentina. This was Nicole's first time preforming at Coachella starting weekend one of the music festival. On both weekends Nicole provided an electrifying performance with a rhythmic and smooth delivery. In addition to Nicole weekend two performance in which she provided her very own rendition of Eminem's classic song called "Lose Yourself". And the supporters seemed to love each part of the rendition. Nicole's supporters were at her set singing and dancing along to each of their favorite songs. Nicole's Coachella performances are only setting the stage to what is to come in the future of this bright artist.
